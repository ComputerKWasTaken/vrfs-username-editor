How It Works

This tool converts a standard hex color to the VRFS 3-digit color format.

  1. Enter your username
  2. Select colors using the preset colors or RGB sliders
  3. For multi-color names, enable additional colors and position them with the cursor controls
  4. The tool automatically converts your colors to the VRFS format
  5. Copy the result and use it in VRFS!
